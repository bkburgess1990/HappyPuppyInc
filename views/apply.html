More About Us

Happy Puppies Inc. is a company founded on the premise that all puppies should be happy! Founded in 1967, we have grown from a grassroots campaign to a multi-billion dollar corporation. In business terms this may not make for a good business model, but here we are.

Our goal has been to find puppies all over the globe who need love - and give it to them! We have a fleet of private jets who scour the planet, picking up puppies and delivering them to our headquarters located in Tacoma(That's our building to the right).
