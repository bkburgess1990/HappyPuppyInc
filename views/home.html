Come Visit Us Today!

We are located in Auburn, Washington. You'll know you found us because you'll be at the only 85-story building in the area! Our dogs can't wait to meet you!

The Adoption Process

We are very selective to whom we allow our dogs to be adopted to. We want to ensure our friend is going to a great home where they will get all the attention they deserve! That said, here are some requirements:

  • Must have a house (please no apartments/condos)
  • Must have a bedroom for your new friend
  • Must have a (minimum) 1-acre yard for playing
  • Must have several hours a day available for attention!

Donations

Our friends need food and new toys! If you're not quite ready to adopt, consider donating a small amount of money or send us some high quality dog food or some toys for them!
